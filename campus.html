About ITM Skills University


- ATLAS SkillTech University is India’s first new-age urban multidisciplinary university. Founded to forge a path to the future, ATLAS is where the confluence of design, technology, strategy, and leadership takes place. We believe in breaking paths and conventions. We are constantly defining, shaping, and redefining the future of education in India right here in the heart of an ever-booming, cosmopolitan, and enterprising Mumbai.

- With India’s National Education Policy 2020 as the cornerstone, ATLAS endeavours to deliver a multidisciplinary education across 3 new-age streams: Design & Innovation, Management & Entrepreneurship and Digital Technology. It has adopted a futuristic model co-created with an international community of scholars, academicians, industry experts, and world leaders in education. Our undivided focus on academic excellence, world-class faculty, state-of-the-art infrastructure, global institutional collaborations and unique corporate connect empower students to succeed in Industry 4.0 and become socially-responsible global leaders.

- Our vibrant community of ATLAS Fellows has gone on to pursue higher education at top-ranked universities in the world and work shoulder-to-shoulder with leading industry stalwarts. ATLAS Faculty and Fellows have collaborated with the best across the world to solve today’s pressing problems. The University’s interdisciplinary approach, industry-integrated experiential learning, global exposure and emphasis on 21st Century Skills ensures that the ATLAS students are future-ready to continue to drive global impact in the VUCA World we live in today. This exceptional student outcome and our goal to inspire the next generation of leaders, innovators and entrepreneurs is ATLAS’ raison d’être.

  • Vision

    With the able support of its advanced programs and technology system and a set of trained faculty members to give students the vital global edge, ITM is steadily moving towards bringing a revolution in becoming a global leader in the education sector. It wishes to create a supportive learning environment that will generate professionals and leaders for some of the best industries in the near future.

  • Mission

    To provide a competitive and professional learning approach that would train and consult students in various disciplines to suit corporate needs.

    To impart futuristic and comprehensive education of global standards with a high sense of discipline, social significance in a serene and reviving environment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Course Curriculum

What should I expect from the MasterCamp in Applied Finance?

The 1-year MasterCamp in Applied Finance, co-designed and delivered by KPMG in association with Masters’ Union, is a fully hands-on and placement targeted programme. During the 12-month duration of the programme, you should expect rigorous industry immersive training inclusive of outcome-driven learning experiences. The programme also comes with an opportunity of a 4-month-long virtual internship for freshers and a live project for experienced professionals.

What should I NOT expect from the MasterCamp in Applied Finance?

The MasterCamp in Applied Finance does not follow a conventional pedagogical approach that is fully focused on theoretical knowledge. The programme curriculum is meticulously structured to enable hands-on learning through real-life projects, internships & apprenticeships. However, you should not expect guaranteed job placements upon completion of 12 months.

Is it mandatory to participate in projects?

Participation in hands-on projects under the mentorship of KPMG’s finance and accounting experts helps you to become industry-ready. It is mandatory because these projects contribute to a significant part of your total credits during the programme.

What does “Hybrid” teaching mode mean?

All the sessions in the MasterCamp in Applied Finance are conducted live & online over Zoom.

To help the folks enrolled in the programme make the most out of their learning journeys - Masters’ Union will arrange for the students to attend the live sessions out of a coworking space across locations in India provided there is a significant number of enrolled students in that location.

We have found this arrangement to be effective for peer learning and forming genuine friendships as you work towards kickstarting your career in Finance.

What type of learning experience should I expect from the programme?

This programme is focused on 100% skill-based learning using the latest practices, real-world assignments and finance case studies like Yes Bank, Demonetization, Fintech, SBI etc. It is set up in an online-offline format so that you can collaborate with your peer group in person and on campus. You also get to leverage the brilliant opportunity of being taught live by the KPMG team as well as the co-op design of the curriculum integrating hands-on academics with real-life projects, internships & apprenticeships.

Is any certification granted at the end of the programme?

Yes. Upon completion of case studies, boot camps, hands-on projects and in-person peer collaborations, you will be granted a Certificate of Graduation – MasterCamp in Applied Finance.

What are the session timings for the upcoming cohort?

The live sessions with the KPMG in India team are held over Saturday & Sunday mornings to match the schedule of working professionals and students.

Career Support

What type of career support should I expect from this programme?

During the programme, you will receive assistance from the Career Services team at Masters’ Union and you will be able to track your career progression with a personalised plan. In addition, you will attend workshops on LinkedIn profiling, personal branding, interview preparation and salary negotiation that will help you amp up for the placement drive. During the process, you will have access to the B-School placement network and internal job portal to apply for jobs with recruiters.

How will my doubts/questions be addressed in an online programme?

As this programme is in a hybrid format, your doubts and questions can be either addressed during online classroom sessions or in-person meetups with your peers. Additionally, you will also share a common forum with the batch where you will be able to get your queries addressed from the instructors or initiate a discussion with your peer group.

Does Masters’ Union or KPMG in India guarantee a job upon the completion of this programme?

No. Neither Masters’ Union nor KPMG in India take any responsibility for your placement in a full-time role or the conversion of the virtual internship into a PPO upon the completion of this programme.

Why is the internship at KPMG in India virtual?

The internship offered as part of the programme is virtual because the KPMG in India team has been working remotely since COVID-19.

Can I apply for an in-office internship with KPMG in India?

Students will be eligible to apply for an in-office internship with KPMG in India, but the selection will happen at the discretion of the KPMG in India team.

What is meant by the integrated virtual internship at KPMG in India?

An integrated virtual internship at KPMG in India will give you the opportunity to directly work on an immersive project supervised by a KPMG mentor. During the virtual internship, you will work in teams of upto 5 students and will meet virtually with your supervisor and team to discuss projects and progress. This valuable work experience during the programme duration will add an extra edge to your CV, which you can further showcase while applying and interviewing for full-time roles.

Will all the students be eligible for integrated virtual internships at KPMG in India?

Students, who maintain over 90% attendance and have a min GPA of 3.0 out of 4.0 will be eligible for the virtual internships with KPMG in India.

Is the virtual internship paid or unpaid?

The virtual internship at KPMG in India an an unpaid one.

Admission Criteria

Who should apply to the programme?

This programme is structured to cater to the learning requirements of undergraduate students as well as young professionals. Freshers currently pursuing B.Com/BBA/BA or equivalent Bachelor’s degree in Finance/Economics/Accounts with a minimum of 50% result are eligible to apply. Additionally, this course is also suitable for working professionals with 1-2 years of work experience, aiming to transition to a career in finance and accounting in industries like Banking, Insurance, Financial Services, Consumer Goods, IT/ITeS, Manufacturing, Telecom, Pharma.

My current role does not include exposure to Finance or Accounting. Does it make sense for me to opt for this programme?

Yes. If you have 1-2 years of work experience and are aiming to transition to a career in finance and accounting in industries like Banking, Insurance, Financial Services, Consumer Goods, IT/ITeS, Manufacturing, Telecom, Pharma, this programme is apt for you. However, a fundamental understanding of Finance and Accounting operations would be preferable.

What is the admission process for the programme and what is the timeline?

Admission to this programme is a 2-step process. The first step is the submission of the online application along with the required documents. Next, the selected candidates will be provided with an offer of admission to the programme.

What is the minimum educational qualification required to take this programme?

You should be an undergraduate fresher currently pursuing a B.Com/BBA/BA or equivalent Bachelor’s degree in Finance/Economics/Accounts with a minimum of 50% result.

Financing & Refunds

What is the programme fee for the MasterCamp in Applied Finance?

The total programme fee for the MasterCamp in Applied Finance is INR 3,20,000 + GST.
However, you can avail no-cost EMI options, starting at INR 16,387/month, through our banking partners.

What is the payment fee schedule?

If you are offered a place on the programme, you will be notified of a specific date by which you must confirm acceptance and pay the non-refundable admissions fee deposit along with a refundable security deposit.

What is the refund policy?

The application fee, admission fee, and tuition fee are all NON-REFUNDABLE.

Can I get more than one scholarship?

No, a candidate is eligible for one scholarship only. It is noteworthy that scholarships are granted according to various criteria depending on factors like the candidate’s application, academic scores, work experience, if any, and performance in the interview.

Is there any deferral or refund policy for this programme?

Currently, we are not offering the option to defer the admission offer. Those who cannot join the programme during the current intake are advised to apply again for the next intake.
