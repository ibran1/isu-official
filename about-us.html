About ITM Skills University


ITM Skills University emphasizes preparing executives or working professionals for everyday business challenges and developing insights and knowledge for strategic decision-making and career advancement.

  • Vision

    With the able support of its advanced programs and technology system and a set of trained faculty members to give students the vital global edge, ITM is steadily moving towards bringing a revolution in becoming a global leader in the education sector. It wishes to create a supportive learning environment that will generate professionals and leaders for some of the best industries in the near future.

  • Mission

    To provide a competitive and professional learning approach that would train and consult students in various disciplines to suit corporate needs.

    To impart futuristic and comprehensive education of global standards with a high sense of discipline, social significance in a serene and reviving environment.

Chairman’s Message

I had the privilege to be at the helm of the ITM Group of Institutions from the very beginning and have seen it to become a great institution as it is today. I can tell that being an educationist, nothing gives you more pleasure than witnessing the careers of our students taking off, emboldened by their experiences at ITM. You are reading this message because you too are looking forward to start something that you hope will transform your life, your career, and even the world! I want to congratulate you for taken the first step towards making that transformation happen.
